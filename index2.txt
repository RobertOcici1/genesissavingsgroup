<h1>Products & Services </h1>
<h2>Shares</h2>
-A share represents a member’s ownership in the Cooperative, with a nominal value set by the Management Committee (minimum 1 share valued at 100 USD).<br>
-Founder Members: Must hold 3–36 shares, not exceeding 10% of total shares and savings.<br>
-Ordinary Members: Must hold at least 1 share, with no maximum limit but subject to the same 10% cap.<br>
-Non-members cannot hold shares.<br>
-Shares earn 11% annual dividends and form part of the non-withdrawable “core capital” but are transferable upon membership termination.
-Shares are not used for loan collateral, but holding the minimum is required for borrowing eligibility.
<h2>Regular Savings</h2>
Members must make regular savings after completing their share contributions.<br>
Savings earn 10% annual interest.<br>
Withdrawals require a 30-day notice.<br>
<h2>Benevolent Fund</h2>
Members contribute UGX 10,000 monthly to a fund covering the member, spouse, and four children under 18.<br>
Benefits: UGX 300,000 upon death of a member/spouse and UGX 150,000 for a child.<br><br>

(b)Salary Loan<br>
